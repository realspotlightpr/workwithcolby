Hire an AI team for less than one salary.

The most expensive part of digital work is often not one employee. It is the gaps between strategy, design, development and delivery.

By Colby Fifer · Updated July 2026

A growing business can easily need a designer, developer, automation specialist, IT troubleshooter and someone who understands AI. Hiring each role separately is expensive. Coordinating several freelancers can be just as costly in time.

The alternative is an embedded operator who can move across those disciplines and keep the work connected.

What the traditional setup hides

A salary is only one line item. Add recruiting, onboarding, management time, software subscriptions and the delays created when work passes from one specialist to another.

The cost is fragmentation.A campaign stalls while it waits for a landing page. The landing page waits for copy. The automation waits for credentials. Nobody owns the complete outcome.

A simpler operating model

My plans use one request queue and one accountable relationship. You can add unlimited tasks, design requests and development work. I focus on the highest priority, ship it, gather feedback and move to the next.

The Operator

Best for one active brand or primary project that needs a steady stream of execution across AI, IT, design and development.

The Growth Partner

Designed for agencies and multi-project entrepreneurs. It is not a fixed comparison-table upgrade. It is a higher-access relationship for managing multiple brands, client projects and strategic priorities at the same time.

Where the savings become growth

  • Fewer handoffs and less project management.
  • No new quote every time the type of task changes.
  • Premium AI and production tools included in the work.
  • Design and development decisions made in the same context.
  • A repeatable rhythm that compounds instead of restarting.

This model is not right for every company. If you need a large department with many simultaneous specialists, build that department. But if you need senior, flexible execution without six figures of overhead, an embedded AI-native partner can be the more useful first move.
